Welcome!

I use digital systems to study eco-evolutionary dynamics at broad spatiotemporal scales. Like most ecologists, I'm fascinated by the vast diversity of life that coexists on our planet. Understanding the evolutionary and ecological processes that generate and maintain this diversity is important both for biology and for machine learning via evolutionary computation. To learn more, check out my research and publications. Code for all of my projects is open source and available on github and slides for all of my talks are available on my cv page (there are some videos there, too!).
